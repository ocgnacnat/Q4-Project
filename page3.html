PLACES TO VISIT IN PALAWAN (Photo Gallery)

The Philippines is a beautiful country with tons of places to take tourist visits. We come from awestuck oceans to friendly-filled lands, offering you with a handful of activities to try out! You'll get more of an idea or two with all the things you can do, and some of the best places in Palawan. Scroll and read!

El Nido Beaches
  • One of the beaches
    got 2 beaches in one!

From surfing to kayaking, the El Nido beaches
will leave you with an extraordinary island-hopping adventure.

Some of the best beaches in El Nido are the
"Nacpan and Calitang Beaches", "Snake Island",
and "Dolarog Beach".

The Coron Bay
  • With "battle" ships that
    sunk beneath the waves.

Eerie as it may seem, wreckdiving is one of the interesting
activities to do in Coron bay.

You can go from appreciating the surface of the wrecks
to entering the vessels itself and discovering what remains
of 1944 within to today.

Taytay Fort
  • Also known as
    "Fuerza de Santa Isabel"
    named by Spaniards.

On the historic side, the TayTay Fort was made
to defend against pirates and slave-raiders in the 1700s.

On the upper levels, you'll find what seems to be a small park.
Best believe you'll also see the cannons they had to defend
themselves, watching over even today.

Iwahig River
  • You'd find them usually
    near mangrove trees.

Grab your camera, because you wouldn't want to miss
the firefly watching in Iwahig River.

Located in Puerto Princesa, you can take a riverboat cruise as
a community activity to witness the dancing fireflies in motion.
